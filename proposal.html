What is this?

This is a project from Web Tech class. My current plan is to make it a history of Bangladesh, where I am from. I am starting with this idea but as I go along with this project, I might make some changes accordingly.

Why?

While working on the history of Bangladesh, I will get the exposure of the history and will get the opportunity to learn more about other countries. There are different opinion on third-war countries. I wanted to make people aware of those countries and the posable ways to help those countries.

For Who?

It is for anyone who is looking to learn more about other countries. My goal is to make the Bangladeshi American community more aware of our background. As a Bangladeshi American, it is hard to connect back to the country I was born in.

Content

The content of this project is still in the process of being created. I want to address the independence of Bangladesh. I want to mentions the ruler of Bangladesh, the culture, and community. As I have mentioned before, I don't have as much knowledge about Bangladesh, I will learn along the way.
